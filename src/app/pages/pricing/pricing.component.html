Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans.

Can I change my plan anytime?+

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.

Is there a free trial?+

The Starter plan is completely free forever. For Pro features, we offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

How does annual billing work?+

With annual billing, you pay upfront for 12 months and receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. You save 2.4 months worth of fees.

Can I have multiple restaurant locations?+

The Enterprise plan supports multiple branches. Contact our sales team and we'll design a custom solution to fit your chain's needs.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major credit/debit cards, and bank transfers for annual Enterprise subscriptions. All payments are processed securely.

Our team is happy to walk you through the right plan for your restaurant.
